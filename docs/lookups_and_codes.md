# Lookups and Codes

This document explains the lookup dictionaries used in the advanced dashboard to convert raw Database identifiers into human-readable labels, and the rationale behind the separation of technical potential curves.

## Resource Identification Codes
The IAC Database tracks fuel types using a combination of stream codes.
- `E1` (Legacy Electricity) was replaced in 1995 by `EC` (Consumption), `ED` (Demand), and `EF` (Fees).
- `E2` corresponds to Natural Gas (MMBtu).

The script `scripts/extract_resource_codes.py` extracts a table of 30 codes from the `IAC_DatabaseManualv10.2` PDF. The resulting JSON acts as the source of truth for the `backend/app/lookups/resource_codes.py` module. 

In addition to labeling features, the python module defines classification helpers (`is_electricity()` and `is_natural_gas()`), which the `advanced_analytics` API relies on.

## ARC Measures (Assessment Recommendation Codes)
The ARC list acts as the taxonomy for industrial energy recommendations. They use a dot-decimal hierarchy (e.g., `2.1111 CONTROL PRESSURE ON STEAMER OPERATIONS`).

The script `scripts/extract_arc_codes.py` parses `ARC List - V21.1.pdf` using regex level inference to extract all ~805 measures, storing their titles and hierarchies. Application Codes (1-4) are also documented to further differentiate end-uses (e.g., "App 1: Manufacturing Process").

## Dual Curves Architecture (Electricity vs. Natural Gas)
The legacy codebase generated a single curve by naively using `yearly_savings` or summing heterogeneous energy data.

To correctly model Cost of Conserved Energy (CCE), the Advanced Dashboard separates the data entirely:
1. **Physical Energy Savings**: Iterates through `pconserved`, `sconserved`, etc., matching the respective `sourccode`. Electricity codes are aggregated as `MWh` (converted from kWh) and Natural Gas codes as `MMBtu`.
2. **Cost Allocation**: Since `implementation_cost` is reported as a single total for a recommendation, the cost is proportionally allocated to the Electricity and Natural Gas channels based on the fraction of *dollar savings* (from `psaved`, `ssaved`, etc.) generated by each resource.
3. **Discrete Curves**: Two curves are generated, avoiding comparing MWh against MMBtu. The UI toggles between the two.
